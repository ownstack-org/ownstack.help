Domains & SSL

Routing custom domains to OwnStack apps and getting them on HTTPS. Most of the time it's two steps: ownstack app domains:add + a DNS A record. Let's Encrypt handles the cert. The detail pages cover every edge.

By task

  • Add a custom domain — apex or subdomain, end-to-end.
  • Let's Encrypt — how the cert flow works, renewal, rate limits.
  • Wildcard certs — DNS-01 challenge, provider hookups.
